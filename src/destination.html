Barcelona

Barcelona is Spain's cultural capital, home to Antoni Gaudí's whimsical architecture and museums. Visitors can explore centuries-old neighborhoods like Barri Gòtic and La Barceloneta beach, as well as take a day trip to Costa Brava, a must-visit for beach bums and history buffs.

Seville

Seville is a romantic city in southern Spain with outdoor cafes, cobblestone streets, horse-drawn carriages, pastel-colored plazas, flamenco dancing, and must-see sites such as the Plaza de España, the Real Alcázar, and the Catedral de Sevilla, the largest Gothic Cathedral of its kind in the world.

Madrid

Madrid is one of the most popular cities in Spain, known for its party-hard reputation and vibrant neighborhoods. It is also home to some of the best art institutions in Europe, such as the Prado Museum and Thyssen-Bornemisza National Museum, and its grandiose architecture makes it the perfect backdrop for a romantic getaway.

Granada

Granada is a unique destination due to its Arabic influence and its history as part of the Moorish Empire. It is home to tapas bars, flamenco venues, and Moroccan tea cafes and bathhouses, allowing visitors to experience two cultures simultaneously. Visitors can also take in the breathtaking local treasures, such as the Alhambra, Sacromonte district, and Sierra Nevada National Park.

Mallorca

Mallorca is a dreamy island getaway with sun-kissed beaches, picturesque small towns, and outdoor pursuits that draw tourists in droves. Revel in the blue waters of Playa de Muro and Cala Llombards, and drive around the striking mountains of Serra de Tramuntana. Take leisurely strolls along Alcúdia's old town and by the water to admire the Palma Cathedral.
